Boxmate

BoxMate will be the perfect solution to managing your box office needs. The application will work in any type of theatre (for example movie theatre or auditorium). The application will be able to manage shows, including multiple showings – each with individual ticket sales. Different seating categories will be available, allowing for different seating prices throughout the theatre. Tickets can be printed and given to the customers.
